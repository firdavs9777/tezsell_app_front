## Privacy Policy Section - User-Generated Content

**Content Moderation & Reporting**

Sabzi Market allows users to report objectionable content including:
- Product listings
- Service listings
- Chat messages
- User profiles

**How It Works:**
1. Users can report content directly from the app
2. Reports are submitted with a selected reason and optional details
3. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 24 hours
4. Appropriate action is taken, including:
   - Content removal
   - User warnings
   - Account suspension or banning

**Data Handling:**
- Reports are stored securely on our servers
- Report data is used solely for moderation purposes
- User privacy is protected throughout the reporting process
